Something We Want To Say

  • The Importance of Pets

    随着社会生产水平的提高，人们生活节奏逐渐加快。快节奏的生活可能常常让我们感到焦虑或迷茫，当我们身边暂时没有可以倾诉的对象时，宠物可以成为我们朋友，给予我们心理上的安慰。
    With the improvement of social production level, people's pace of life is gradually accelerating. The fast-paced life may often make us feel anxious or confused.
    When there is no object to talk to around us for the time being, pets can become our friends and give us psychological comfort.

    • Brief Introduction of This Web

      宠物是人类社会中的重要角色，有关宠物的知识甚多，但是相关网站的数量和质量无法得到保证。因此我们整合了几个网站上的信息，去除了广告和营销号等无效信息，
      并且增加了一些新功能，用更加清晰简约的网站风格为用户提供更好的搜索体验。
      Pets are an important role in human society. There is a lot of knowledge about pets, but the quantity and quality of relevant websites can not be guaranteed.
      Therefore, we have integrated the information on several websites,removed invalid information such as advertising and marketing numbers, and added some
      new functions to provide users with a better search experience with a clearer and simpler website style.

  • Space for Improving

    因为关于宠物可参考的网站较少，因此部分宠物的信息无法全部展示。此外，这个网站还可以创建一个养宠者论坛，为大家交流养宠经验、分享养宠趣事搭建一个更好的平台。
    Because there are few websites available for reference about pets, the information of some pets cannot be fully displayed. In addition,this website can also create
    a pet breeder forum to build a better platform for everyone to exchange pet experience and share pet fun.

    • The Original Purpose

      生活中宠物被遗弃、流浪街头甚至失去生命的现象屡见不鲜，令人痛心。这些养宠人常常因为宠物不如自己想象中可爱、宠物怀了孕、养宠成本太高、自己没有精力等原因将
      他们抛弃，因此我们希望建此网站，旨在让准备养宠的人对养宠形成更客观的认知，以帮助他们做出更理智的决定。
      It is sad that pets are abandoned, wandering in the streets and even lose their lives. These pet breeders often abandon their pets because they are not
      as cute as they think,they are pregnant, the cost of pet rearing is too high, and they have no energy. Therefore, we hope to build this website to enable
      people who are ready to pet to form a more objective understanding of pet rearing, so as to help them make more rational decisions.
